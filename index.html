JobFit

  • Free, private and instant — everything runs in your browser; nothing is uploaded, saved or tracked, and you never need an account.
  • Works for any field and any country — you bring the job posting, so it fits every market.
  • Shows your match, the exact keywords worth adding, your resume's health, and can build a starter resume from scratch if you have no experience yet.

  • It matches keywords, not meaning — a human recruiter reads nuance it cannot, so treat the score as guidance, not a verdict.
  • It does not search or apply to jobs for you; you paste the posting yourself.
  • It cannot guarantee you will pass a company's real ATS or land an interview.
  • Resume-norm and country tips are general orientation, not legal or immigration advice — always verify with official local sources.
  • Every suggestion is a starting point you must make true — it never invents experience for you.
